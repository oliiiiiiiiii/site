## At the table

Food vocabulary becomes easier to remember when it is attached to a situation rather than a long isolated list. Begin with a few words you can use in a real exchange.

| Español | English | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| el pan | bread | Quisiera pan, por favor. |
| el agua | water | Una botella de agua. |
| la cuenta | the bill | La cuenta, por favor. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Practice in phrases">
  Learn **quiero café** as one unit before memorizing *quiero* and *café* separately.
</Callout>

## Tiny dialogue

> — ¿Qué desea?
>
> — Quisiera un café y un bocadillo, por favor.

The goal is not perfect recall on day one. It is recognizing the shape of a useful exchange.
