## Essential words

| Español | English | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| el desayuno | breakfast | ¿Qué quieres para el desayuno? |
| la comida | food / lunch | La comida está lista. |
| la cena | dinner | Cenamos a las ocho. |
| el agua | water | Quisiera un vaso de agua. |

<Callout title="Usage note" type="tip">
  In Spain, **la comida** often means the main midday meal, not only “food” in general.
</Callout>

## At a restaurant

Useful sentences:

- **Quisiera pedir…** — I would like to order…
- **¿Qué me recomienda?** — What do you recommend?
- **La cuenta, por favor.** — The bill, please.

## Quick practice

Translate this sentence: “I would like water and a coffee, please.”

> Quisiera agua y un café, por favor.
