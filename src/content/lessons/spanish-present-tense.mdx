## Regular endings

For a regular verb, remove **-ar**, **-er**, or **-ir**, then add the matching ending.

| Person | hablar | comer | vivir |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| yo | hablo | como | vivo |
| tú | hablas | comes | vives |
| él / ella | habla | come | vive |
| nosotros | hablamos | comemos | vivimos |

<Callout title="Remember" type="note">
  Spanish often omits the subject pronoun because the verb ending already identifies the subject.
</Callout>

## Examples

```text
Estudio informática.
Vivimos en Taipéi.
¿Comes carne?
```

## What comes next

After the regular pattern feels familiar, add a small set of high-frequency irregular verbs such as **ser**, **estar**, **ir**, and **tener**.
