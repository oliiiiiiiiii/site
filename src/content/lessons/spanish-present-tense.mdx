## The basic pattern

Spanish verb endings carry information about who performs an action. For a regular **-ar** verb such as *hablar*, remove the ending and add a new one.

```text
hablar → habl- → hablo, hablas, habla
```

## A compact table

| Person | Ending | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| yo | -o | hablo |
| tú | -as | hablas |
| él / ella | -a | habla |
| nosotros | -amos | hablamos |

<Callout type="note" title="Pattern before exceptions">
  Build confidence with regular verbs first. Irregular forms become less intimidating once the regular system feels automatic.
</Callout>

## Practice prompt

Write three sentences about what you do during an ordinary week. Keep each sentence short enough that the verb remains the focus.
